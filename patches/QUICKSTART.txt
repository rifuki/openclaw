OpenClaw Multi-Bubble Patch Quickstart (Portable)

1) Status check (no changes)
python3 ~/.openclaw/patches/apply-multibubble-dist-patch.py --status

2) Dry-run
python3 ~/.openclaw/patches/apply-multibubble-dist-patch.py --dry-run

3) Apply patch
python3 ~/.openclaw/patches/apply-multibubble-dist-patch.py

4) Restart gateway
systemctl --user restart openclaw-gateway
systemctl --user status openclaw-gateway --no-pager -l

5) Test
Send `/reset` from WhatsApp, then test a 3-4 paragraph group reply.

When to rerun:
- after `openclaw` update
- after reinstall
- after node version/toolchain change
- on new server/device setup
