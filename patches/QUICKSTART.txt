OpenClaw multi-bubble quickstart

1) Audit
python3 ~/.openclaw/patches/apply-multibubble-dist-patch.py --status

2) Safe apply (with rollback on error)
python3 ~/.openclaw/patches/apply-multibubble-dist-patch.py --strict

3) Restart gateway
systemctl --user restart openclaw-gateway
systemctl --user status openclaw-gateway --no-pager -l

4) Verify in WhatsApp
- send /reset
- trigger 3-4 paragraph group reply
- confirm separate bubbles
